This page includes the original sylvester library. Hit execute to benchmark a multiply of two 1024x1024 matrices. (You will have to hit continue when it asks if you want to stop the long running script.)


Open up firebug or the console to test it out
example code: 

    var one = $M([[1,2], [1, 2 ]]);
    var two= $M([[1,2], [1, 2 ]]);
    var result = one.multiply(two)
    alert(result.elements);
